Too-Day 
" A Social Community App. "


Main Problem

In Today’s Generation,

We all are connected to each other through social networking apps, and always stay up to date about the things that are Happening around us through news channel, newspapers or from some Fellow Friends.
But is it true, For example, do you really know the people who lives in your city, or the latest things that are going on around you ,in your city ?
According to a survey we found out that, 70% people are not aware about there city, and has to face alot of complications. For instance, one of my friend lost his dog few days back. He tried his level best to look for his dog, but he wasn’t able to make it and finally, at the end he left the hope. This is just one instance there are much more like this road blocks, special events, accidents, robberies , weather, transport etc. Which happen in our city on a daily basis and we are not aware about them.
So, We decided to make a digital platform, where people can come and know about the latest feeds happening in their city instantly or a community, where people can share text ,videos, photos or opinions with their city mates. As this will assist them to know more about their surrounding or to work as a community.

Our Solution

Too-Day,
Is an online Social Community App, which help local people of a city to share information (Photo, Video, Text or Opinions) with each other.
In addition to that, there is no need of sending requests, a person who is living in whichever city will automatically get connected to that city page with a connection to all the city members and that’s just by a single click.
What’s more , If a user moves from one city to another , He will be directly linked to that city page. Thus, he will always stay Up-to-Date about the things that are happening in the city or around him, and can also contribute by uploading Content related to that city. Moreover, People have full rights to like, dislike, comment or report any feed, which help us to prioritize the feed on Too-day, and if any feed is reported abusive or violent our team instantly react to that post and block that user from posting any content on Too-day thus maintaining Privacy.
